# /connect

Bridge two domains, projects, or ideas that Jay has been circling separately. Finds non-obvious connections across the vault — the kind of synthesis that only becomes visible when you can see everything at once.

## Usage
/connect [domain A] and [domain B]

Example: `/connect SellerFi and AI agents` or `/connect DeFi and seller financing`

## What to do when /connect is run:

1. Ask Jay which two domains/projects/ideas to connect (if not provided)
2. Read all notes related to Domain A from the vault
3. Read all notes related to Domain B from the vault
4. Perform deep cross-analysis looking for:
   - Shared vocabulary or concepts appearing in both
   - Problems in A that solutions from B could address
   - Audiences, markets, or use cases that overlap
   - Mental models Jay uses in both areas
   - Timing/sequencing relationships (does A enable B? Does B validate A?)
5. Generate 5 concrete connection insights — not generic, specific to Jay's actual notes
6. For each connection, suggest one actionable idea or experiment

Output format:
```
## Connection Map: [Domain A] × [Domain B]

**Shared Concepts:** [terms/ideas appearing in both]

### 5 Non-Obvious Connections

1. **[Connection title]**
   - In [A]: [what Jay wrote]
   - In [B]: [what Jay wrote]
   - The bridge: [insight]
   - Action: [one thing to try]

...

### The Synthesis Opportunity
[The big idea that emerges from combining both — 2-3 sentences]

### What to build/write/explore next
[concrete recommendation]
```
